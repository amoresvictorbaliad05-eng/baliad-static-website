Introduction to HTML5

HTML5 is the fifth and current major version of HyperText Markup Language (HTML),
the standard language used to structure and present content on the World Wide Web.
Officially released in 2014, it was designed to improve the web by supporting modern multimedia,
enhancing mobile responsiveness, and providing better semantic structure without relying on third-party plugins like Adobe Flash.
